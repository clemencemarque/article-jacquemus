Jacquemus, examining a success story
through its offer and positioning



Jacquemus is the brand of the designer Simon Porte Jacquemus, at barely 10 years old the brand is already a great success and has become one of the most trendy French ready-to-wear houses of the moment. The 30-year-old designer Simon Porte Jacquemus never stops to surprise and reduce the distance with his digital community.

Simon is influenced and attached to his region, his family, his " Bombas" and his "Gadjos" to which he dedicates entire collections. It's innovative in the world of Fashion and Luxury that a designer puts his family and friends in front of the light.

It is through its actions that Jacquemus differentiates itself from other brands, it wants to show that the world of Fashion gets along with a sunny and more convivial way of life. It is through its catwalks, its campaigns and its philosophy that Jacquemus transmits its way of Fashion.

In the name of jacquemus

#Jacquemusathome

To represent the authentic spirit of the brand, the creator wanted to honor one of the women in his life. Liline (here in the pictures), Simon's grandmother, she became for a moment the muse for the new Jacquemus campaign for the summer 2020 collection. The designer took advantage of it by deploying the #Jacquemusathome referred to the moment of quarantine related to the health crisis, it shows the example of the rules to be respected by the public, at the same time shows a new way to communicate on fashion.



This campaign was entirely released on the social media Instagram and received a great success with about 3 174 posts from fans including the #Jacquemusathome . The creator Jacquemus knows how to show spontaneity, proximity and freshness in his communication through the campaigns he undertakes. Moreover, as the #Jacquemusathome campaign shows, he is a man who does not hesitate to involve friends and family. He delivers precious moments that he lives. Simon is a creator who wishes to remain intimate and simple for his community by showing himself as the little boy from the south of France with a big smile.

The brand's marketing strategy

A digital native brand

" Instagram is my brain, unfiltered. It's very personal. It's a whole.
Sometimes I post things I shouldn't put on, but I can't help it. "

Jacquemus is a "digital native" brand. These web-born brands have the ability to fully capture and understand the behaviour of new audiences because they have used the Internet as a listening organ. It's by paying attention to what it's said on social media that they have identified available e-commerce niche markets. Jacquemus is one of those brands that are totally user centric, which means that they are globally connected to their targets.
Also, far from the perfect images of traditional luxury homes, Jacquemus imposes a cool, simple, provincial style. Nature pictures are combined with fashion shows and festive evenings with friends. It is through this free tone that Simon expresses himself, he doesn't skimp on familiar words, emojis and hashtags.

Instagram as a diary

The power of Jacquemus is its Instagram page, like a diary of the creator and the brand. The designer stages himself in his own creations, with members of his family or his creative teams. Behind this idea, we find a communication that is more direct and warm, a simple tone that knows how to make itself accessible. It shows a desire to democratize luxury clothing and accessories to make it more human and social.

The heart of the digital stars

The brand is notable for its skillful manipulation of social media. The marketing strategy is to acquire customers through this channel. The brand has over 2.6 million subscribers on Instagram that it can target and interact with. Jacquemus has seduced some of today's biggest stars who influence Instagram users such as Emily Ratajkowski (here in the picture), Rosalia, Beyonce, Bella Hadid, Kylie Jenner... He loves these icons like a part of his family. If he likes them so much, it's because these great digital influencers made such a major impact on the growth of the brand's reputation over the last four years. In the same way, if the young designer has caught the eye of these famous idols, it's because he has also been able to create strong pieces that which do not leave his customers and the media indifferent.
